About Us

   The Mixed System’s founders came together from the various sectors of the telecom industry to form a group to provide the most comprehensive ICT service availability to both Telecom operators, vendors and ICT operators. Our experts have been involved in the telecommunication industry since 1992 and very capable of delivering the high quality of service and support require by ICT’s business today.

   Mixed System Co., Ltd. were established in 2006. Under goal of customer oriented organization in Telecom and IP Technology Industry. We have fully confidence in offering excellent service to your organization by providing ICT project implementation both inside and outside plant, including planning, survey, installation, testing & commissioning, integration, cut-over, Project Management and maintenance for various of customers. We have expanded our Civil Work business to Full Civil Construction Work business.

   We have highly competent engineers who have very strong background in Telecommunication Network experiences from Operators & Vendors side and Construction Work experiences from Real Estate Business's Owners.

   Our team has long experience on Fiber Optic Cable transmission and IP transport network including MPLS, DWDM, SDH, Microwave, Civil Work, BTS, Node-B installation & commissioning and RF Optimization. Our team also has experiences on Civil Construction Work. We also had an alliance company in the region to support our work in each region.
